Admissions

Bonamuva Primary School welcomes new learners where space is available. Below is the current status of vacancies and the admissions procedure.

Current Vacancies

Grade R – 2 spaces available

Placement is subject to documentation and interview. Please contact the school office to enquire and reserve a place.

Grade 4 – 1 space available

Placement will be assessed based on previous school records and an entrance assessment.

How to apply

  1. Download and complete the official admissions form (available at the school office).
  2. Submit the form together with the learner's birth certificate, latest school report (if applicable), proof of residence and vaccination records.
  3. Attend a short interview/assessment with the school if required.

Online application (request only)
