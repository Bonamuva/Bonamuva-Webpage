Welcome to Bonamuva Primary School

A proud primary school in Eshowe committed to academic excellence, strong values and community partnership.

We provide a supportive, disciplined and nurturing environment where learners discover their talents and develop the skills necessary for future success.

Admissions & Apply

Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive and stimulating learning environment where every child is empowered with knowledge, values and skills to thrive academically and socially.

Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in primary education in the Zululand region — producing compassionate, confident and capable learners who contribute to a better society.
