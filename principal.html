Message from the Principal

It is with great honour and a deep sense of responsibility that I extend my warmest greetings to all who visit Bonamuva Primary School — The Star of Hope. Our school is committed to providing a nurturing and disciplined learning environment where each learner is encouraged to achieve academic excellence and personal growth.

At Bonamuva Primary School, we uphold the highest standards of integrity, respect, and dedication. We believe that education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the broader community — a partnership that shapes the future of our learners and contributes to the advancement of society.

Under the guidance of our dedicated educators, every learner is given the opportunity to discover their talents, develop critical thinking skills, and embrace the values that will prepare them for future success. Together, we strive to ensure that Bonamuva Primary School continues to be a beacon of excellence in the Eshowe community.

I take this opportunity to thank all parents, staff members, and community partners for their unwavering support and commitment to our shared vision of quality education for all.

Miss M.P. Ntuli
Principal, Bonamuva Primary School
