About Bonamuva Primary School

Bonamuva Primary School is a proud educational institution serving learners from Grade R through Grade 7 in the Eshowe community. We combine strong academic programmes with a rich offering of extracurricular activities that nurture character, creativity and collaboration.

Our teachers are dedicated professionals who foster learner-centred classrooms, where curiosity and critical thinking are encouraged. The school emphasises literacy and numeracy, coupled with life-skills and values education to produce well-rounded learners.

Facilities and Programmes

  • Well-maintained classrooms and a central library.
  • After-school clubs: soccer, choir, creative arts and a developing coding club.
  • Structured learner support and counselling services.
  • Community outreach and environmental stewardship projects.

Our Values

Integrity, respect, diligence and community. We believe that education is a shared responsibility and value strong partnership with parents and local stakeholders.
